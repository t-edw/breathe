❃breathe.

welcome to breathe, an interactive painting game designed to calm. to begin, then simply mouse over the square below to see the picture appear. if you are on a device with a touch screen, you can tap in the square below to see the image. to get another image and reset, you can press the clear button. there are also several sound options available. try them out by clicking the button with the sound you'd like to hear. if you would prefer not to hear anything at all, click silence and the sounds will stop. to change the size of the brush, use the slider above. if you would like to paint a specific picture, you can click on the icon that appears below the canvas.

Canvas is not supported
